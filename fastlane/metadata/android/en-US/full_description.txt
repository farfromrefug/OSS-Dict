**Aardict** is an offline dictionary reader. It is fork of Aard 2 with new Material design and update features

**Features**

* Lookup queries are punctuation, diacritics and case
insensitive.
* Bookmarks and History
* Dictionary Management
* Article Appearance: add your custom style sheets
* Math: Mathematical formuals are scalable, styleable, beautiful on any screen
* Random Article
* Volume Buttons Navigation
* Fullscreen Mode
* Clipboard Auto-Paste
* External Link Sharing
